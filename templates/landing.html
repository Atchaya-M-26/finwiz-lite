How FinWiz Lite summarizer works

Step 1

Upload your statement

Upload a bank statement or financial PDF from your device. The app extracts text from each page and detects transactions.

Step 2

Auto‑categorize spending

Simple rules classify transactions into salary, rent, EMI, food, shopping, insurance and more, so you see where your money goes.

Step 3

Generate clear summary

The dashboard shows total income, expenses, net savings and a human‑readable summary you can download as a PDF.

Frequently asked questions

What types of files can I upload?

FinWiz Lite is optimized for bank statements and financial PDFs. Other PDF documents may work, but results are best when the text is clearly selectable, not scanned images.

Is my financial data safe?

Your files are stored only in your account area. They are used to compute summaries and analytics for you, and you can delete them from your dashboard at any time.

Does the tool change any numbers in my statement?

No. The app only reads amounts and descriptions to calculate totals and categories. It does not modify the original document.

Can I download or share the summary?

Yes. After processing, you can download a PDF summary from your dashboard, and Pro plans can export detailed CSV data.

Will more languages and formats be supported?

The current focus is English PDFs, but future updates will add support for more formats and Indian languages.
